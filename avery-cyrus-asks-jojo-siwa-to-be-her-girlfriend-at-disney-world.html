Avery Cyrus Asks JoJo Siwa to Be Her Girlfriend at Disney World

Publish date: 2024-07-03

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

JoJo Siwa via Instagram

Reading Time: 2 minutes

After denying allegations about the status of their relationship, JoJo Siwa and Avery Cyrus are now an official. The TikTok creator popped the question to Siwa during their trip at Disney World.

Avery Cyrus, JoJo Siwa are Officially Dating

During their recent trip to Disney World, Avery Cyrus mustered up the sweetest way to ask JoJo Siwa to be her girlfriend. Cyrus snuck off with Siwa’s father and created a cute banner using spray paint. The final product featured “JoJo Be My Gf?” in various colors, with hearts surrounding.

On the same day, the Siwa family (plus Cyrus) went to Disney World. Following their filled day, Cyrus and JoJo Siwa took a water taxi adventure, to see the multi-colored sign.

The former Dancing With the Stars contestant was evidently surprised, but nevertheless didn’t hesitate to say yes. In Siwa’s footage, the viewer can hear Cyrus thanking Siwa for her reaction.

“Of course. My God. I’d be insane if I said no,” Siwa replied. “You’re the best girl in the world.”

In the first clips of the YouTube vlog, Cyrus was already hinting about elevating the relationship to another level. Siwa, on the other hand, wasn’t aware until the moment finally happened. No matter how you feel about this couple, you have to admit, this was a pretty cute moment.

Siwa was Emotional While Celebrating the Special Moment

Even after the surprise, Siwa was still crying out of happiness. The two went out with Siwa’s parents to celebrate night.

“There’s a bunch of people staring at me and I’m crying,” Siwa said. “I’m emotional.”

The intimate link between Cyrus and Siwa is not a secret to the public. Before confirming their relationship, the two had been seen getting close on TikTok. Although the Dancing With The Stars alum made it clear that she and Cyrus weren’t official yet, they were never denial of their feelings with each other.

This past August, Siwa’s longtime girlfriend, Kylie Prew confirmed that they broke up earlier in the summer. For fans of Siwa, it’s seemed like a whirlwind since her breakup. Weeks after Prew’s confirmation, Cyrus split with her girlfriend of two years Soph Mosca. Very soon after Cyrus’s split, she was seen creating content with Siwa. Due to the quick timeline and apparent overlap, many fans believe Siwa and Cyrus had their sights on each other for quite some time.
