Skip Brittenham - Variety500 - Top 500 Entertainment Business Leaders

Publish date: 2024-05-25

Brittenham is one of Hollywood’s legal power brokers, with loyal decades-long clients including Harrison Ford, Tim Allen, Tom Selleck and Henry Winkler. He cofounded his firm with Ken Ziffren in 1978 and went on to steer many industry-shaping deals, such as the launch of Pixar, David Ellison’s Skydance Media and Christopher Meledandri’s Illumination Entertainment as well as the split of DreamWorks into two entities. Currently on his roster are big-name actors (Eddie Murphy, Bruce Willis, Drew Carey, Michael Keaton), top-tier directors (Ridley Scott, Shawn Levy, Andrew Stanton), prolific producers (Joe Roth, Nina Jacobson), execs (Dana Walden, Kevin Feige, John Lasseter, Toby Emmerich, Jim Gianopulos, Tom Rothman) and production companies (Liz Murdoch’s Locksmith Animation, Reel FX, Working Title, Pioneer Pictures).
