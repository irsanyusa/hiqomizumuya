Celia Muoz Receives Support from Terry Fator, Ana-Maria Mrgean Amid 'AGT' Las Vegas Residency

Terry Fator and Ana-Maria Mărgean recently visited Sin City to watch fellow ventriloquist Celia Muñoz. The trained opera singer is currently a part of America’s Got Talent Las Vegas residency Superstars Live.

Terry Fator, Ana-Maria Mărgean Watch Celia Muñoz in Las Vegas

As AGT continues to impress with Superstars Live, the show’s alums didn’t miss to render support to their fellow artists. Fator and Mărgean recently took the time to show up as audiences at Luxor Las Vegas where the 75-minute variety show plays.

Grateful for the support, Muñoz show appreciation for the two via Instagram. According to the AGT finalist, having these fellow ventriloquists in the audience was a privilege.

“Today we had in @agtsuperstars the honor to have @terryfator and @anamariamargean.official at the audience,” she wrote. “Thanks for coming h to see the show! You are amazing.”

Margean responded in the comment section of the post, stating that Muñoz was “amazing.” The young ventriloquist also praised Superstars Live itself, concluding that it was a “great show.”

It isn’t new seeing AGT ventriloquists manifesting support for one another. Muñoz herself went to New York-New York Las Vegas to watch Fator do his show. Indeed, it feels nice to witness how connected these alums are even though they’re technically competitors in the industry.

Munoz Says Superstars Live was a Surprise

Before she was officially enlisted in the lineup of performers for Superstars Live, Muñoz didn’t think it was possible. Nonetheless, after jumping in despite the uncertainty, the ventriloquist successfully realized that she could make it. Muñoz reflected on this on Instagram, during the early days of her residency.

“Moments before going on stage and doing something you have never done,” she wrote. “That was my feeling before my audition in @gottalenttv and after in @agt . After a year of @agt now I am performing in @agtsuperstars in Las Vegas . What can I say!? Be open , life keep surprising.”

Clearly, his fans are proud of how far Muñoz has come. One supporter even commented that she deserved such big milestone.

America’s Got Talent Presents Superstars Live is a night delight in Las Vegas at Luxor Hotel & Casino. Basically, a line-up of talented AGT winners, finalists, Golden Buzzer acts and fan-favorites grace the stage to thrill the crowd. Currently, Muñoz performs  Kodi Lee, Light Balance, Deadly Games, The Silhouettes, Aidan Bryant, Mervant Vera, Pack Drumline, Human Fountains, and host Mike E. Winfield.
