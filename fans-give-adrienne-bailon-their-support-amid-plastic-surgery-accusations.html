Fans Give Adrienne Bailon Their Support Amid Plastic Surgery Accusations

TV personality and singer Adrienne Bailon has inspired an outpouring of support from fans after an Instagram user accused her of having plastic surgery. The criticism stemmed from a recent post in which Bailon shared a compilation video of herself showcasing various bikini looks while on vacation.

Fans Rally Behind Bailon After Plastic Surgery Accusation

Bailon recently posted an Instagram video showcasing her vacation bikini looks. She received an outpouring of compliments, including from celebrities like Khloé Kardashian and Meagan Good. However, one commenter accused her of having plastic surgery, writing “Wow, you have a great surgeon.”

Bailon cleverly replied that her “surgeon” is @nyricanmama – the Instagram handle of her mother, Nilda Felix. Fans loved Adrienne’s comical response and supported her against the plastic surgery accusations.

One fan wrote that Bailon’s beauty is “homegrown,” while another called the critical commenter “insecure” and said they’ll never have a woman like Bailon. Other fans agreed she got her looks from her mom and praised her “greatest clap back.”

In a surprise twist, the critical commenter returned to apologize for the plastic surgery speculation. Hopefully it makes them think twice before commenting on people’s bodies.

“I apologize, it’s such a typical thing for celebrities that I assumed this was the case. Again I apologize and God bless,” The critic wrote.

Adrienne Bailon Addresses Plastic Surgery Talk With Positivity

The singer and TV personality spoke with ET backstage at the 2023 Daytime Emmy Awards about the recent plastic surgery speculation. Rather than get upset, Bailon took the comment as a compliment.

She admitted it was hard to stay in shape after having a baby, so hearing someone suggest she paid for surgery made her feel like she must look great. Bailon attributes the speculation to the insecurities many women have about their self-image. While the critic didn’t see her cellulite, she still appeared perfect to them.

Bailon welcomed a baby boy, Ever, via surrogate in 2022. Reflecting on the past year, she described it as “crazy but super special.” Motherhood has been an adjustment, like when her son accidentally head-butted her the morning of the Emmys. But she laughs about the joys and challenges of parenting.

In the coming year, Bailon looks forward to more of a routine of staying home and working. She also plans to record Latin-inspired party music in her home studio for a potential 2025 release. While unsure of a music comeback, she enjoys making fitness a priority and wants to spread more positivity through her platform.
