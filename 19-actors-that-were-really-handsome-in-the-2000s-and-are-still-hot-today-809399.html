19 Actors That Were Really Handsome in the 2000s, and Are Still Hot Today

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Sometimes, you turn the TV on and see a movie you watched in childhood. And you see the actor, but you can’t remember his name. So, you take the phone and check out the year the film was released and who played the main roles.

Matt Damon

East News, Moritz Thibaud / ABACA / Abaca / East News

The guy we remember from The Departed, Good Will Hunting, and The Bourne Identity, continues to star in movies. One of the recent films he was in is called Stillwater, where he played the lead character.

Mark Wahlberg

EVERETT COLLECTION / East News, face to face / FaceToFace / REPORTER / East News

The star of The Departed, The Italian Job, and Transformers has tried a lot of things: acting, playing in a band, and producing a series on HBO. And he has also created his own charity foundation.

James Marsden

This is the guy that didn’t listen to his father and drove a red BWM in Interstate 60. He still has a lot of fans around the world. And he will play the prince in Disenchanted.

Chris Klein

The cutest guy from American Pie took a break from his career, and then returned in The Flash. And he mostly appears in series that are broadcast on streaming services.

Tobey Maguire

ASSOCIATED PRESS / East News, BG004 / Bauergriffin.com / MEGA / Mega Agency / East News

When you see his face, do you have the urge to do the Spider-Man dance? We found out that Tobey was out of the loop for quite some time due to his nasty behavior during the production of the final Spider-Man film. Now, the actor often assumes the role of producer on the films he’s interested in.

Nathan Fillion

Some people remember him as the fighter for justice, Mal, from Firefly. Or as Richard Castle from the popular series Castle. One of the more recent movies you can see him in is Suicide Squad 2.

David Boreanaz

The actor that played the charming vampire Angel in Buffy and the cool FBI agent Seeley Booth in Bones also produces movies and series, writes scripts, and takes part in TV shows.

Orlando Bloom

2 years ago, Legolas and Will Turner became a father for the second time. He and Katy Perry gave their daughter a beautiful name, Daisy Dove. The actor’s career is developing well and fans are looking forward to movies and series with Orlando Bloom.

Ben Whishaw

He played the perfumer that created an incredible scent and drove people crazy. He has recently appeared in the Bond film, No Time to Die.

Zac Efron

The star of High School Musical and Baywatch appears in a lot of projects and voices cartoon characters. And his fans are discussing his appearance changes and multimillion-dollar deals with buying and selling property.

Christian Bale

Bale’s career includes roles in The Prestige, The Dark Knight, and many other wonderful films, and it’s gotten a new boost. He portrayed the main villain in Thor: Love and Thunder where he was accompanied by Chris Hemsworth, Tessa Thompson, Chris Pratt, and Natalie Portman.

Colin Farrell

The actor that portrayed Alexander the Great, has recently appeared in a new version of Batman.

Antonio Banderas

ASSOCIATED PRESS / East News, JORGE GUERRERO / AFP / East News

The Spanish actor, famous for the role of Zorro, also produces perfumes under his name. Banderas made his loyal fans happy when he appeared in the action film, Hitman’s Wife’s Bodyguard. He chases the main characters and gives them a hard time.

Dana Ashbrook

Bobby Briggs from Twin Peaks is no longer the hooligan he was 20 years ago. But he hasn’t lost his charm. The actor appears in movies and series and shares the photos of the best moments of his life on social media.

David Tennant

Possibly the greatest Doctor of all time is 51 years old. He has 5 children, he’s still a popular actor, he voices movie characters, and he even produces miniseries.

Billy Zane

Billy Zane, the fiancé of Rose in Titanic, is only remembered for this role by many people, but in fact, he’s been in many other projects. Dozens, in fact. If you want to see Billy on screen, you can watch one of the most recent movies with him called Final Kill.

Nicholas Cage

Meme hero and cool guy Nicholas actively stars in movies and produces them. And if you think that there aren’t many new movies with him, it’s because he sometimes appears in series too. You can already see a new film with him called Willy’s Wonderland.

Jake Gyllenhaal

The guy with sad eyes that stole millions of people’s hearts around the world is still a very popular actor. Last year, you could see The Guilty, a remake of the Danish thriller where Jake played the main role.

Jared Leto

It’s been almost 20 years since the photo on the left, but it seems that Jared just grew a mustache to look at least a little older. He doesn’t just not age, but he also continues to tour with his band Thirty Seconds to Mars. And his work in movies and series is planned several years in advance.

Which of these actors do you like the most?
