Vanessa Williams (Singer and Actress)

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Profession: Singer and Actress

Biography: Vanessa Williams came to fame when she became the first person of African American descent to win the Miss America contest in 1983. She was forced to give up the title after a scandal erupted when Penthouse published unauthorized nude photographs of her.

Williams released her debut R&B album "The Right Stuff" in 1988 with the hit single "Dreamin'". Other successful albums have followed including "The Comfort Zone" (1991) featuring the No. hit "Save the Best For Last".

As an actress Williams has starred in roles in films such as "Soul Food (1997) and on television as in "Ugly Betty" (2006-10) alongside America Ferrera, and for which she was nominated for three Primetime Emmy Awards.
