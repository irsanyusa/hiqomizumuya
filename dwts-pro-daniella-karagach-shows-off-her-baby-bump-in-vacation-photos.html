'DWTS' Pro Daniella Karagach Shows Off Her Baby Bump in Vacation Photos

Publish date: 2024-05-22

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Daniella Karagach via Instagram

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Dancing With The Stars professional dancers Daniella Karagach and Pasha Pashkov can’t contain their excitement for the arrival of their first child. The couple surprised their DWTS castmates about the pregnancy news before the season finale, and they’re now one step closer to welcoming their first child.

Daniella Karagach Shared 20 Week Baby Bump Update

While on a vacation together in a Grand Resort at Riviera Maya, south of Cancun, Mexico, Karagach and Pashkov took the chance to relax with their unborn child. They also went and captured special moments during their vacation. Many of the photos captured shows Karagach’s growing baby bump.

From the caption of the post, Karagach shared that their baby is very active and energetic. The many kicks coming from her child is a good sign of having no problems and being healthy. The featured photos from the post, show them together with some friends enjoying their time beside the beach.

“It’s amazing how much and how rapid my body is changing. I’m truly loving every second of it,” Karagach wrote.

Following their pregnancy announcement in November, the couple revealed in a joint Instagram post that they are expecting a daughter. During Karagach’s birthday celebration, they shared a video showing the event including when they learned the baby’s gender.

Karagach Recently Celebrated 14 Years of Being Together with Pashkov

The couple also recently celebrated 14 years of being together, marking the milestone with sweet tributes to their love story. Karagach and Pashkov married in July 2014, and in July of the previous year, they marked their eighth wedding anniversary.

“We’ve created our lives together for the past 14 years and looking back, we’ve been through some crazy times which only made us stronger and love each other harder,” Karagach said.

Pashkov then replied with a post of his own, and a short but meaningful message to his wife. They celebrated the milestone in the same party they held for Karagach’s birthday, making it a celebration of three life events. Announcing the gender of their baby, Karagach’s birthday, and lastly, their 14 years of togetherness, all celebrated in one night.
