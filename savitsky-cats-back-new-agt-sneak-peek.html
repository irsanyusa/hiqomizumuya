Back in May, the very first performance we were treated to on the America’s Got Talent Season 13 premiere was the audition of the delightful Savistky Cats. Now these fun felines are back to show off some more tricks!

The animal act will compete on tomorrow night’s episode of Judge Cuts, featuring guest judge Martina McBride. Us Weekly has shared a preview of their performance, in which we see part of their circus-themed routine.

There’s been a pattern going on with these AGT “sneak peeks”: all of the acts who’ve gotten one so far this season have advanced to the next round. Now, I don’t believe we’ve had any exclusive clips released by Us Weekly prior to today’s, so the situation could very well be different this time and this act may not end up moving on. But I think there’s a good chance this is an indication the Savitsky Cats did indeed advance to the quarter-finals, so I’ll be keeping an eye on them while the episode airs tomorrow night at 8/7c on NBC.
