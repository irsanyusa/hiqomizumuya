George Michael (Pop Superstar) - On This Day

Publish date: 2024-06-24

Full Name: Georgios Kyriacos Panayiotou
Profession: Pop Superstar

Biography: One of the world's best-selling music artists George Michael rose to fame as a member of the music duo Wham!.

Michael's 1987 debut solo album, "Faith" sold more than 20 million copies worldwide.

He had seven #1 singles in the UK and eight #1 hits on the Billboard Hot 100 in the US, including "Careless Whisper" and "Freedom! '90".

Posthumously inducted into Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2023.

Born: June 25, 1963
Birthplace: London, England, United Kingdom

Generation: Baby Boomer
Chinese Zodiac: Rabbit
Star Sign: Cancer

Died: December 25, 2016 (aged 53)
Cause of Death: Suspected heart failure
