Full Name: Nelly Kim Furtado
Profession: Singer-Songwriter

Biography: Nelly Furtado is a Canadian singer-songwriter and record producer. She is a multi-platinum and Grammy Award-winning artist who has sold over 20 million albums worldwide.

In 2000, Furtado released her debut album Whoa, Nelly!, which featured the single "I'm Like a Bird". The single won her a Grammy Award for Best Female Pop Vocal Performance and was nominated for Song of the Year.

Following the release of her album Folklore in 2003, Furtado released Loose, which was her most successful album to date. It was certified six-times platinum in the United States and produced the hit singles "Promiscuous," "Maneater," and "Say it Right."

Furtado has released five studio albums, one compilation album, one extended play, and thirty-five singles. She has also collaborated with numerous other artists, including Justin Timberlake, Timbaland, and The Black Eyed Peas.
