Ryan Reynolds Paid a Birthday Tribute to Sandra Bullock With a Memorable NUDE Scene

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Ryan Reynolds and Sandra Bullock have a history together. They not only starred in the movie The Proposal together, but they also filmed a nude scene. And Reynolds shared that priceless clip on Bullock’s birthday with a comical caption.

Fernando Allende/Broadimage/Broad Image/East News, Invision/Invision/East News, © Emojipedia

The Deadpool actor sent warm birthday wishes to Bullock by reminiscing about their 2009 rom-com The Proposal. In the film, Reynolds portrays a dedicated assistant who reluctantly agrees to marry his ambitious boss (Bullock) to prevent her deportation to Canada. The nostalgic birthday message brought back fond memories of their on-screen chemistry and the lighthearted humor that endeared them to fans.

And by sharing a nude scene from the movie, Reynolds created his co-star’s most unique birthday wish ever. “Happy Birthday to the inimitable and stunning Sandra Bullock! For your birthday this year, I got us both intimacy coordinators. And an HR department. And clothing?” he said in his Instagram post.

Bullock openly discussed the scene on the About Last Night podcast, where she shared her experience during filming. Despite Reynolds’ playfulness, the talented actress expressed a genuine sense of “camaraderie and safety” throughout the process.

She said, “We had a closed set, which means no one’s allowed to be on there, just hair and makeup coming into fluff and hide. Ryan and I have these flesh-colored things stuck to our privates. We couldn’t really move.”

Their experience highlights the professionalism and respect shared between the co-stars during the making of the film. And the result was a fun, memorable movie that we can all go back and watch over and over again.
