Angelina Jolie Hires Her 15-Year-Old Daughter as Her Assistant and Causes a Debate Among Fans

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Hollywood superstar Angelina Jolie has recently made headlines by hiring her 15-year-old daughter as her personal assistant. The decision sparked a heated debate among fans, raising questions about the fine line between a celebrity’s private and professional life.

Jolie has three daughters.

Angelina has 6 kids from her previous marriage to Brad Pitt. The actress has 3 beautiful daughters — Shiloh and Vivienne are biological, and one adopted — Zahara. Jolie says, “I have 6 very individual human beings in my home. I’m so excited about all the different stages, feelings and curiosities they go through. Why wouldn’t you be?”

She hired Vivienne as her assistant.

Dylan Travis/ABACA/Abaca/East News, Dylan Travis/ABACA/Abaca/East News

Angelina Jolie is now a producer on a new Broadway musical based on the novel The Outsiders. The actress hired her 15-year-old daughter as her manager for the upcoming production. Jolie assures, “She’s very thoughtful and serious about theater and working hard to best understand how to contribute.”

Some people praised her decision.

zz/John Nacion/STAR MAX/IPx/Associated Press/East News

Some fans were happy for them. One user wrote, “She’s going to work with her mom, that’s awesome good for her.” Another praised Angelina, “An amazing mother.” Some were also excited to see the musical, “Ugh The Outsiders! I’m so excited! One of my favorites.”

Others thought it was unnecessary.

Some didn’t hesitate to call Vivienne a nepo baby. One user wrote, “Such nepotism. Way to take a job from someone that could have actually used it to help their career.” Someone added, “A real assistant should be a person who knows what they are doing, a professional, someone who gets paid. If not, it’s exploitation.” Some people also claimed that Jolie used her kids as PR.

The term “nepo babies” refers to successful celebrities’ kids and has often been seen in headlines and on TV. We also have some articles devoted to such children. In this one, we reveal how Cindy Crawford helped Kaia Gerber succeed in the fashion world.
