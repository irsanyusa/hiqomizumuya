ETSRL Music, Nasboi's Umbrella Cocktail Party: Spectacular night of music

Publish date: 2024-06-20

The night, held in resplendent style, became a harmonious symphony of talent and glamour.

Nasboi's rhythmic masterpiece, "Umbrella," released on November 24, 2023, has swiftly ascended the ranks, becoming the anthem of choice for music enthusiasts. With over 25 million streams on various DSPs and dominating the airwaves as the most-played song in Nigeria, Nasboi's musical journey under ETSRL Music has undeniably struck a resounding chord.

ADVERTISEMENT

To commemorate this milestone, ETSRL Music organised an extravagant and colourful Cocktail Party, extending gratitude to the multitude who contributed to the song's success and its resounding impact on the music scene.

The star-studded event saw the glitterati of the Nigerian entertainment industry grace its premises. From music maestros like PSquare, Wande Coal and Falz to comedic genius AY Comedy, charismatic skit maker Cute Abiola, the effervescent Tomike, and the lively MC Lasisi Elenu – the party was a constellation of luminaries.

ADVERTISEMENT

In an exclusive interview with MTVbase, Dr. Wisdom, the CEO and visionary behind ETSRL Music, expressed immense delight at the overwhelming response to their invitation. He acknowledged the tremendous support received and hinted at more remarkable ventures ahead for Nasboi, affirming that "Umbrella" merely marks the starting point of his musical odyssey.

ADVERTISEMENT

Nasboi, in his element, graced the stage, captivating the audience with a stellar performance of "Umbrella" and a medley of his other hits. Teasing the crowd, he announced a forthcoming collaboration with some top Nigerian musicians.

ETSRL Music emerged as a formidable force in the music industry, guided by the visionary record label owner - Dr. Wisdom. The label envisions itself as not only a dominant force in Nigeria but also a global music powerhouse, nurturing and projecting musical talents to international acclaim.

As the night concluded with an exuberant after-party, the vibrant ambience and jubilant atmosphere resonated with the attendees, marking the ETSRL Music and Nasboi's Umbrella Cocktail Party as a monumental celebration of musical ingenuity and boundless success.
